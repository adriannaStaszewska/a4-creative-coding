Nobel Prize Laureates Help
This is a visualization of Nobel Prize Laureates dataset created by Adrianna Staszewska for CS 4241.
You can interact with the visualisations in the following ways:
  • Use sliders to change years in the "Gender differences" and "Country of Birth of Laureates"
  • Use checkboxes to filter data
  • Hover over elements (dots or ares in the mao) to get the information about the laureate
